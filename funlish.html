About Funlish

Funlish concentrates on teaching curricular and extra curricular English literature and language to students of primary, middle and high school. Out-of-the-box methods are used to enable students to have fun with English.

Their comprehension, reading, writing and communicative skills are strengthened through regular quiz, creative writing and elocution contests, battle of books, enactment of literary pieces, language games and many more. The sole objective is to let the children enjoy reading English without dreading the language and explore its tremendous potential in the modern world.

About TCPDI

TCPDI is an NGO that works towards the fostering of peace in the community. It runs a school for primarily the socio economically backward children of a suburb of Kolkata. The objective is to impart education for peace in the violence prone sectors.

In this field we have been partnering with students of Duke University USA for two years now.
